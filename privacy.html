Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Tapsworth is a macOS utility that plays mechanical keyboard sounds when you press keys. Your privacy is important to us, and Tapsworth is designed to be completely private by default.

Data Collection

Tapsworth does not collect, store, or transmit any data. Specifically:

  • No keystroke logging. Tapsworth detects key press events solely to trigger sound playback. It does not record, store, or transmit which keys you press.
  • No personal information. Tapsworth does not collect names, emails, identifiers, or any personal data.
  • No analytics or tracking. Tapsworth contains no analytics frameworks, telemetry, or tracking code.
  • No network access. Tapsworth does not connect to the internet. It operates entirely offline.
  • No third-party services. Tapsworth does not integrate with any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Accessibility Permission

Tapsworth requires macOS Accessibility permission to detect system-wide key press events. This permission is used exclusively to trigger sound and haptic feedback in real time. Key events are processed in memory and immediately discarded — nothing is written to disk or sent anywhere.

Data Storage

Tapsworth stores only your selected preferences (keyboard switch type and enabled/disabled state) locally on your Mac. No data ever leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Tapsworth does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
